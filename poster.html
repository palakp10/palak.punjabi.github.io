Wine in the Age of Enlightenment

What is this project about?

This project reimagines a wine bottle as a metaphor for royalty and wealth, drawing from the Neo-Classical Period's emphasis on symbolism. The bottle’s packaging design reflects the era’s principles, featuring intricate drapery, balanced symmetry, linear motifs, and a sophisticated color palette. The packaging seamlessly blends elegance with historical inspiration, embodying the grandeur of the period. This design was further transformed into a poster, where the packaging’s visual language celebrates the timeless sophistication of Neo-Classicism.
