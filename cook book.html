What is this project about?

The Oreo Cookbook is a playful and interactive recipe collection inspired by the theme of "hide and seek." Targeting kids with a Gen-Z flair, it features imaginative typography explorations and visually dynamic layouts. The project underwent multiple iterations to refine the balance between creativity, readability, and engagement, showcasing the importance of iterative design and storytelling. This cookbook blends aesthetics and functionality to create an immersive and delightful culinary experience.
